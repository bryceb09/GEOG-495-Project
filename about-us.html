Purpose

Throughout my time spent at OSU I have been given the same question over and over again with many different answers. Even though there are definitions for geography I believe that the true definition is tailored to the individual. Each of us have walked different paths in life and to each person geography means a different thing. It is through this idea of the individuals definition that I created this project to allow each person to experience what geography is to them since geography is built on observing and exploring.
